ABOUT

Namratha Shivaraju

Welcome to paint no jutsu.

I am Namratha Shivaraju from Bengaluru, India. I am 20 years old. I am a Btech, computer science engineering student, presently in 3rd year at INDIAN INSTITUTE OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY DHARWAD.

My love towards anime, manga and art is what lead to the creation of paint no jutsu.

The story behind creation of Paint no jutsu is that one day I had the urge to rewatch Naruto. The episode "The new chapter begins: The chunin exam" is where Gaara makes his first appearance (and Gaara is my favourite character). The same evening I wanted to paint something and I decided to try painting any one of the Naruto characters. 7 hours later, my first anime character painting, Gaara, came to life. After being extremely satisfied with the painting, I decided to make more and share it with people of same interest. That's when I started an anime art page, @paint_no_jutsu, on Instagram for my paintings.

This website is not just about my paintings. I also post lastest anime related news,manga suggestions, anime suggestions, anime sites and my views on the on-going animes(with spoiler alert)

Thank you for visiting Paint No Jutsu. Join me in this journey of discovering amazing content.
